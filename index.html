food insecurity in oakland

what is food insecurity

Food insecurity in the bay area has been a problem since segregation was born.In the bay area over 870,000 of people face food insecurity due to food droughts and food deserts.the bay area is one of the most expensive places to live in the united states,due to this more and more predominantly POC residents are being pushed into poverty and food insecurity.An area code should not define how long you get to live.My app intends to create a reliable way to get food and to find food sources near you for cheaper prices with the use of maps and links directing you to a close source of healthy food.
